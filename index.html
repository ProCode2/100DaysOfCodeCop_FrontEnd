#100DaysOfCode - COP

An initiative to reduce the abuse of #100DaysOfCode hashtag on twitter by reporting the tweets that uses the hashtag but does not serves the core purpose of #100DaysOfCode, instead is used solely to gain twitter engagement.

Reprts

What Do We Do?

We record tweets that twitters users think is an inappropriate tweet for #100DaysOfCode and ask people to report the tweet if they think the same. We're just getting started, and understand the vulnerabilities of this process and working hard to make it better. We'll do our best to not let people use this service for their personal matters.
